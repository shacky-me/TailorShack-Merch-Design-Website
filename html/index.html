About TailorShack

TailorShack wasn't born in a classroom, but on a dusty road trip across the country. I was (and still am!) constantly inspired by the unique styles and local flair I encountered everywhere I went. Seeing people express themselves through clothing sparked a desire to create a platform where anyone could bring their own creative vision to life, no matter their artistic background.
